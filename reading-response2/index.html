My Website is a shifting house

Laurel Scwults

  • There are millions of ways to make a website, and the abundance is daunting.
  • On corporation website, user’s happiness is not the primary focus.
  • Website is a physical space-“creating a world” with rules.
  • “Clarity is one of many possible intentions for a website”
  • “A website creator becomes both author and architect simultaneously”
  • On the internet no one is looking out for you.
  • A Website can be:
      • A room: comforting, flexible feeling
      A shelf: nothing too heavy, lighter, simple
      A plant: grow on their own, gradually grow, not to be rushed.
      A garden: own ways each season, less output and more stirring around on input, life is about balance.
      A puddle: appear after rainstorms, temporary collection, evaporates over time.
      A thrown rock that’s now falling deep into the ocean: you’ll have to maintain, find a rock and throw it with ideas.
  • “If a website has endless possibilities, and our identities, ideas, and dreams are created and expanded by them, then it’s instrumental that websites progress along with us.”
