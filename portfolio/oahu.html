Oahu Tour Brochure

This brochure design is a tour of Hawaii, Oahu. I often see the tourist brochure on the street but never found them appealing. Therefore, I wanted to create a tourist brochure that does not include any commercial purpose and designheavy. I picked out famous locations and foods in Oahu. I chose to use the colors that represent and remind of Hawaii.
